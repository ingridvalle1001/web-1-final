Hello!

My name is Ingrid Valle. I am an undergraduate student at Eastern Washington University. I will graduate with my Bachelor's in Design and UX Certification summer of 2021. I am a small business owner, I love to create art using a multitude of mediums, and practice photography in my free time. I love doing poster design and other graphic design work including, but not limited to: layout design, image editing, branding packages, merchandise and apparell design, and typography. I have several years of experience with Adobe Creative Suite and have recently been building my knowledge of coding languages (HTML, CSS, JavaScript). Welcome to my website!
